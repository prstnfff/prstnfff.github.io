I'm a data scientist with Amazon.com, based in Seattle, Washington.

Prior to joining Amazon, I was a research assitant at Research Improving People's Lives , a data-driven policy lab based in Rhode Island. I graduated with Bachelor of Arts degrees in mathematics and economics from The University of Chicago in 2015. See my Experience page for more details on my background and skillset.

Outside of work, I enjoy distance running, exploring Seattle by bike, as well as hiking and bouldering. I also enjoy expanding my skillset, especially around data visualization and storytelling.
